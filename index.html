Who is Roland BELIOU?

Born on September 13, 2001 in Po,a city in Burkina Faso, Roland Beliou began his primary education in 2007 at the Wend Poui school complex in Ouagadougou. Poui school complex in Ouagadougou.in CM2, he took part in the entrance exam for the 6th grade at the Prytane Miliatire de Kadiogo. He went on to study at the Lycee Privee Emergence C, graduating with honors in 2021. He was awarded a scholarship to study in Morocco, where he completed a two-year preparatory course at the Ecole Nationale des Sciences Appliquees de Fes. In 2023, he began his engineering studies in Genie Mecanique et Systemes Automatises.

Roland BELIOU is passionate about aeronautics, aerospace, automobiles, in short, anything to do with mechanics, which is why he chose this profession. His hobbies include sport, music, programming, industrial design with Solidworks and Catia software.


In addition, he was part of a team coding a website as part of an end-of-cycle preparatory project.
